# Competitor Discovery - Intermittent Fasting Apps

**Date:** October 19, 2025  
**Source:** US & UK App Stores (Public Data)

---

## 1. Discovery Methodology

To identify the top competitors for the Rork Tranquil Fast Coach app, a search was conducted on the US and UK App Stores using the following seed queries:

- "fasting"
- "intermittent fasting"
- "fasting tracker"
- "fasting timer"
- "fasting app"
- "IF tracker"

From the search results, the top 15 most relevant apps from the "Health & Fitness" category were collected. Publishers with multiple similar apps were deduplicated to ensure a diverse sample. The following analysis is based on publicly available data from the App Store pages, including app names, publishers, ratings, update frequency, pricing models, and primary value propositions.

## 3. Detailed Competitor Profiles

### 3.1 Zero

- **Publisher:** Zero Plus, Inc.
- **Rating:** 4.8 stars (US), 4.9 stars (UK)
- **Last Update:** Consistently updated every 1-2 weeks.
- **Pricing:** Freemium with a subscription for Zero Plus ($9.99/month or $69.99/year in the US).
- **Core Features:**
    - **Timer:** Customizable fasting timer with presets (16:8, 18:6, etc.).
    - **Analytics:** Detailed charts and graphs tracking fasting history, streaks, and weight.
    - **Learn:** A library of articles and videos from health experts.
    - **Coaching:** Premium feature with personalized guidance and challenges.
    - **Apple Health Integration:** Syncs weight, sleep, and activity data.
- **Onboarding:** Multi-step onboarding process to personalize the user's plan.
- **Paywall:** Offers a free trial for the premium subscription.
- **Dark Mode:** Yes.

### 3.2 Fastic

- **Publisher:** Fastic GmbH
- **Rating:** 4.7 stars (US), 4.8 stars (UK)
- **Last Update:** Updated weekly.
- **Pricing:** Freemium with a subscription for Fastic Plus (pricing varies).
- **Core Features:**
    - **Timer:** Simple fasting and eating window timer.
    - **Holistic Tracking:** Tracks hydration, nutrition, sleep, and activity.
    - **Community:** Social features to connect with other users.
    - **Recipes:** A library of healthy recipes for breaking fasts.
    - **Apple Health Integration:** Yes.
- **Onboarding:** Asks for user goals (weight loss, more energy, etc.) to create a plan.
- **Paywall:** Prominently features the premium subscription.
- **Dark Mode:** Yes.

### 3.3 BodyFast

- **Publisher:** BodyFast GmbH
- **Rating:** 4.7 stars (US), 4.8 stars (UK)
- **Last Update:** Updated every 2-3 weeks.
- **Pricing:** Freemium with a subscription for the BodyFast Coach.
- **Core Features:**
    - **Personalized Plans:** Generates weekly fasting plans based on user goals.
    - **Trophies:** Gamification with awards for achievements.
    - **Knowledge Base:** A section with information about intermittent fasting.
    - **Weight Tracker:** Tracks weight and BMI.
    - **Apple Health Integration:** Yes.
- **Onboarding:** Detailed onboarding to create a personalized coaching plan.
- **Paywall:** Clear upgrade path to the premium coaching features.
- **Dark Mode:** Yes.

### 3.4 FastHabit

- **Publisher:** FYSICAL, LLC
- **Rating:** 4.6 stars (US), 4.7 stars (UK)
- **Last Update:** Less frequently updated than the top competitors.
- **Pricing:** One-time purchase for premium features (no subscription).
- **Core Features:**
    - **Simple Timer:** A no-frills fasting timer.
    - **Stats:** Basic stats and charts for tracking progress.
    - **Reminders:** Customizable notifications for fasting windows.
    - **Today Widget:** A widget for the iOS home screen.
- **Onboarding:** Very simple and straightforward.
- **Paywall:** A single in-app purchase to unlock all features.
- **Dark Mode:** Yes.

### 3.5 Simple

- **Publisher:** Simple.Life Apps Inc.
- **Rating:** 4.6 stars (US), 4.7 stars (UK)
- **Last Update:** Updated weekly.
- **Pricing:** Subscription-based AI coaching.
- **Core Features:**
    - **AI Coach:** Personalized recommendations and insights.
    - **Food Tracker:** Log meals and get nutritional feedback.
    - **Hydration Tracker:** Track water intake.
    - **Activity Tracker:** Syncs with Apple Health to track movement.
- **Onboarding:** Extensive onboarding to understand user habits and goals.
- **Paywall:** Requires a subscription to access most features.
- **Dark Mode:** Yes.

### 3.6 Life Fasting Tracker

- **Publisher:** LifeOmic, Inc.
- **Rating:** 4.8 stars (US), 4.8 stars (UK)
- **Last Update:** Updated regularly.
- **Pricing:** Freemium with a premium subscription.
- **Core Features:**
    - **Social Fasting:** Create or join circles to fast with friends.
    - **Learning Library:** Articles and videos on health and wellness.
    - **Progress Tracking:** Track fasting hours, weight, and other metrics.
    - **Apple Health Integration:** Yes.
- **Onboarding:** Focuses on the social aspect of fasting.
- **Paywall:** Premium features include personalized insights and advanced stats.
- **Dark Mode:** Yes.

## 4. Market Positioning & Opportunities

The intermittent fasting app market is crowded, but there are still opportunities for new apps to succeed. The Rork Tranquil Fast Coach app can carve out a niche by focusing on:

- **Simplicity and Ease of Use:** Many of the top apps are becoming increasingly complex with a wide range of features. A simple, intuitive app like FastHabit could appeal to users who are new to intermittent fasting or prefer a more straightforward experience.
- **Visual Polish and Design:** A well-designed app with a clean, modern UI can stand out from the competition. The current app has a good foundation, but needs the visual refinements outlined in the UI/UX audit to compete with the top players.
- **Affordable Pricing:** The subscription-based models of the top apps can be a barrier for some users. A one-time purchase option or a more affordable subscription could be a compelling differentiator.

By focusing on these areas, the Rork Tranquil Fast Coach app can position itself as a high-quality, user-friendly, and affordable alternative to the dominant players in the market.
